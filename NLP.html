Course Decspription

This course provides a comprehensive overview of artificial intelligence (AI), exploring its fundamental concepts, methodologies, and applications. Designed for beginners, it covers key topics such as machine learning, neural networks, natural language processing, and computer vision. Students will gain insights into how AI systems are designed, trained, and deployed in real-world scenarios.
